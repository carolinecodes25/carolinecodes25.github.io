Research

2024 - 2025

VR Gaming on Eliciting Empathy for the Blind

Designed a 3D Virtual Reality game in Unity using binaural sound to create an equal playing field for visually impaired and seeing individuals. The goal was to investigate the effects of simulation quality on eliciting lasting empathetic response for visually impaired peoples, while also created a game that can be enjoyed by all regardless of seeing ability- giving access to the community aspect of gaming to those typically excluded by visual disability. This research was inspired by my father who in his later years struggled with macular degeneration.
